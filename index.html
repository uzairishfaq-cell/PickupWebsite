Our Story

A Neighbourhood
Bistro with Heart

Nestled in the vibrant heart of New Brighton, The Olive Tree Bistro is a beloved neighbourhood gem where fresh, seasonal produce meets inspired cooking. We believe extraordinary meals are born from simple, honest ingredients — sourced locally and prepared with genuine passion.

From our celebrated Sunday roasts to our innovative daily specials, every dish is crafted to delight. Just two minutes from the Floral Pavillion Theatre — the perfect dining destination before or after a show.

Why Guests Love Us

The Olive Tree
Difference

What sets us apart — from the sourcing of every ingredient to the warmth in every welcome

🌿
Locally Sourced

We partner with the finest farms and suppliers across Wirral and Merseyside, bringing seasonal freshness and honest provenance to every plate.

🍷
Curated Drinks

A thoughtful wine list, signature cocktails, and a warm bar atmosphere. Whether you prefer a crisp Sauvignon or a bold red, we have your perfect match.

👨‍🍳
Passionate Kitchen

Our chefs take genuine pride in every dish. From the famous Sunday roast to our legendary sticky toffee pudding, every recipe is made with care.

🎭
Pre-Theatre Dining

Just 2 minutes from the Floral Pavillion Theatre — the ideal spot for a wonderful meal before or after the show, with special pre-theatre deals available.

👨‍👩‍👧
Family Friendly

A warm, welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home. Our attentive staff make every guest feel genuinely cared for from arrival to farewell.

Exceptional Value

Restaurant-quality food at neighbourhood prices. Our guests consistently remark on the outstanding value — fine dining without the eye-watering bill.
